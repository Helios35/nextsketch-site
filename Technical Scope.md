## **Technical Scope**

**Site Scope (Phase 1):**

* Single-page website

* Scrollable layout

* Designed to expand into multi-page later

**Primary Purpose:**

* Positioning

* Qualification

* Conversation initiation (not lead capture)

**Stack:**

* React / Next.js acceptable

* Developer discretion allowed

**CMS:**

* Lightweight CMS for copy updates

* No heavy content modeling required

**CTA Flow:**

* “Conversation qualifier” first

* No immediate sales form

* No newsletter

* No free audit

**Explicit Exclusions:**

* Testimonials

* Portfolio galleries

* Service lists

* Blog / thought leadership

* Case study grids
